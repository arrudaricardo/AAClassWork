Smashed Cucumber Salad

Description

A refreshing summertime treat, that is great as an accompaniment to a meal.

A different recipie here

Ingredients

  • Cucumbers
  • Garlic
  • Corriander
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Chili oil
  • Black vinegar

Directions

  1. Smash the cucumber, and cut into bite-sized pieces
  2. Smash the garlic, and mince
  3. Marinate the cucumber in a pinch of salt for 15 minutes, then drain the liquid
  4. Season the cucumbers with the raw garlic, sugar, chili oil, black vinegar, and salt to taste
  5. Garnish with chopped corriander, and serve cold
